🚨 What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cybercrime where attackers trick you into giving up personal information like passwords, bank details, or OTPs by pretending to be a trusted source (like your bank or a government agency).

📧 How to Identify Phishing Attacks

  • ⚠️ Unfamiliar or misspelled email addresses
  • 🔗 Suspicious or shortened links
  • 📢 Urgent language like “Act Now” or “Your account will be closed”
  • 📎 Unusual attachments or pop-ups asking for credentials

✅ Stay Safe with These Tips

  • 🔒 Never share OTPs or passwords online
  • 🔍 Always verify the sender’s email or number
  • 💻 Use updated antivirus & browser
  • 🧠 Think before you click!
